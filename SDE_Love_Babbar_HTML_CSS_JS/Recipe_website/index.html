Egg Korma

A rich Mughlai-style curry where tender boiled eggs simmer in a creamy, mildly spiced onion-cashew gravy. Perfectly balanced with warmth, aroma, and depth.

Ingredients:

  • 6 boiled eggs
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

250/-

Gulab Jamun

Soft, melt-in-the-mouth milk dumplings soaked in warm saffron-cardamom syrup, offering delicate sweetness and classic festive indulgence.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 4-5 cardamom pods, crushed
  • A few saffron strands
  • Oil or ghee for frying

150/-

Kadai paneer

A bold, flavorful North Indian dish featuring paneer cubes cooked with bell peppers, tomatoes, and aromatic spices in a signature kadai masala.

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

300/-

Sevaiyan Kheer

A creamy and comforting vermicelli pudding simmered in milk, sweetened gently, and enriched with nuts and cardamom.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup vermicelli (sevaiyan)
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds, cashews, pistachios)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 tbsp ghee

280/-

Shahi Tukda

A royal dessert of golden fried bread soaked in saffron-infused rabri, garnished with nuts for a luxurious flavor and texture.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp saffron strands
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Ghee for frying

350/-
